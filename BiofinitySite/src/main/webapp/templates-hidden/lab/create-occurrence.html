Type[*]:
Recorded By[*]:  A list (semicolon-separated) of names responsible for recording the occurrence, with the primary collector or observer first.
Record Number[*]:  An identifier given to the occurrence at the time it was recorded. Often serves as a link between field notes and the occurrence.
Sex[*]:  The sex of the individual(s) represented in the occurrence. Examples include: "female", "hermaphrodite", "8 males, 4 females".
Life Stage[*]:  The age class or life stage of the individual(s) at the time the occurrence was recorded. Examples include: "egg", "eft", "juvenile", "adult", "2 adults 4 juveniles".
Reproductive Condition[*]:  The reproductive condition of the individual(s) represented in the occurrence. Examples include: "non-reproductive", "pregnant", "in bloom", "fruit-bearing".
Behavior[*]:  A description of the behavior shown by the subject at the time the occurrence was recorded. Examples include: "roosting", "foraging", "running".
Disposition[*]:  The current state with respect to the collection identified in the collection code/ID. Examples include: "in collection", "missing", "voucher elsewhere", "duplicates elsewhere".
Establishment Means[*]:  The process by which the individual(s) represented in the occurrence became established at the location. Examples include: "cultivated", "invasive", "escaped from captivity", "wild", "native".
Preparations[*]:  A list (semicolon-separated) of preparations and preservation methods. Examples include: "skin; skull; skeleton", "whole animal (ETOH); tissue (EDTA)", "fossil", "cast", "photograph", "DNA extract".
Individual ID[*]:  An identifier for the individual(s) represented in the occurrence meant to accommodate resampling. Examples include: "U.amer. 44", "Smedley", "Orca J 23".
Individual Count[*]:  The number of individuals present at the time of the occurrence.
Status[*]:  A statement about the presence or absence of a taxon at a location. Examples include: "present", "absent".
Catalog Number[*]:  An identifier (preferably unique) for the record within the data set or collection. Examples include: "2008.1334", "145732a", "145732".
Other Catalog Numbers[*]:  A list (semicolon-separated) of alternate catalog numbers for the occurrence. Examples include: "FMNH:Mammal:1234", "NPS YELLO6778; MBG 33424".
Details[*]:  A reference (publication, URI) to the most detailed information available about the occurrence. Examples include: "http://mvzarctos.berkeley.edu/guid/MVZ:Mamm:165861".
Remarks[*]:  Comments or notes about the occurrence.

[*] denotes reference to Darwin Core terms for an occurrence.
