Drag the marker on the map to automatically set the latitude and longitude of this location.

Latitude[*]: ° Expressed in decimal degrees (DD), ranging from -90.0 to 90.0. Alternatively, use degrees:minutes:seconds (DMS):
     °  '  "  
Longitude[*]: ° Expressed in decimal degrees (DD), ranging from -180.0 to 180.0. Alternatively, use degrees:minutes:seconds (DMS):
     °  '  "  

Lookup location details such as elevation, municipality, county, state/province, and country based on the current latitude and longitude.

Elevation[*]:   If available, the approximate elevation of the location.
Continent[*]:
Country[*]:   The country or major administrative unit in which the location occurs.
State/Province[*]:   The next smaller administrative region than country (state, province, canton, department, region, etc.).
County[*]:   The next smaller administrative region than state/province (county, shire, department, etc.).
Municipality[*]:   The next smaller administrative region than county (city, municipality, etc.).
Locality[*]:   The specific description of the place. More specific than other city/municipality, county, etc.
Water Body[*]:   The name of the water body in which the location occurs.
Island[*]:   The island on or near which the location occurs.
Island Group[*]:   The name of the island group in which the location occurs.
Remarks[*]:   Comments or notes about the location.

[*] denotes reference to Darwin Core terms for a location.
