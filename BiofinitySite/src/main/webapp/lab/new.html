My Labs: New Lab - The Biofinity Project

Create a new lab

If you are currently managing a database of biological data and would like to begin to use My Labs by The Biofinity Project to manage data, you should register your organization as a lab. The following forms will guide you through this process. There will be a several day delay between submission and approval for your lab request as members of our team verify your organization and create a new lab for you.

Join an existing lab

If you are working with a group that has already registered a lab with The Biofinity Project, you can request to join the lab and begin to collaborate with researchers. The following forms will guide you through the process of registering your account and requesting access to the existing lab. There may be a delay as the manager of the lab reviews your request and processes the approval.
