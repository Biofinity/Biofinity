My Labs by The Biofinity Project

Welcome to the My Labs feature of The Biofinity Project. With My Labs you can use the robust data framework provided by The Biofinity Project to manage and interact with your own lab data sets, replacing your current database solution! Using My Labs, you can publish data, share results within your lab, and contribute to The Biofinity Project database.

In order to use My Labs, you must be logged into The Biofinity Project. Log in requires a Google Account. Click the 'Log In' link at the top of the menu bar to access My Labs. If this is the first time you are accessing this feature we will walk you through a short series of setup questions. If not, you will be taken to your lab.
